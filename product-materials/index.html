Series at a glance

From low-flow air paths to high-flow piston modules.

Specifications shown are representative series ranges. Final performance depends on voltage, motor, material, port, duty cycle, tubing, and operating load.

01 series

BD-01 miniature air / vacuum

Small brushed diaphragm platform for portable equipment, medical modules, sampling paths, and compact pneumatic assemblies.

  • Approx. dimensions: 45 × 24.5 × 25.5 mm
  • Low-flow compact format
  • Air and vacuum configurations

02 series

BD-02 compact diaphragm

Balanced air, vacuum, and liquid platform for compact OEM devices where tubing size and installation space are constrained.

  • Flow range: 1–3.5 L/min
  • Pressure range: 100–150 kPa
  • Vacuum range: 40–60 kPa; ID 2.2 / OD 4.0 mm

03 series

BD-03 medium-flow diaphragm

Medium-flow air and vacuum platform with brushed, brushless, and multi-wire control variants for analytical, medical, and automation equipment.

  • Flow range: 3–7 L/min
  • Approx. dimensions: 64 × 30.4 × 49 mm
  • 3-wire speed-control option

04 series

BD-04 high-flow diaphragm

Higher-flow platform for stronger pressure or vacuum reserve, with air, vacuum, and liquid configurations.

  • BD-04A: 13–17 L/min; 240–260 kPa
  • BD-04V: −76 to −80 kPa vacuum class
  • BD-04W: 1.5 L/min; 101 × 72 × 40 mm; 305 g

07 liquid series

BD-07W diaphragm liquid pump

Compact liquid pump for water, cleaning, transfer, dosing, and pressure modules with vibration-isolated mounting options.

  • Maximum pressure: up to 10 bar
  • Port size: ID 4.0 / OD 6.5 mm
  • Flow, voltage, and wetted materials configurable by application

07 piston series

BD-07 piston air / vacuum

Oil-free piston platform for higher pressure, stronger suction, rehabilitation, beauty equipment, leak detection, and robotics.

  • Single head: up to 45 L/min; ≥6 bar
  • Double head: up to 80 L/min; ≥7 bar
  • Negative pressure: −0.085 to −0.095 MPa

08 series

BD-08 mini compressor

High-flow oil-free piston/compressor family for oxygen equipment, spray boosters, 3D printing, environmental monitoring, and portable tools.

  • Pressure range: 1–7 bar
  • Air flow range: 40–80 L/min
  • Single-head, double-head, and continuous-duty options

Accessory

Silencers & fluid-path parts

Silencers, tubing, valves, filters, shock absorbers, and controllers help complete an OEM air or liquid module.

  • Use to reduce noise and stabilize routing
  • Match connector, pressure, and medium compatibility
  • Validate the complete pump plus accessory assembly
