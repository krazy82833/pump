How to choose a category

  • Use diaphragm air pumps for compact pressure, vacuum, and clean airflow.
  • Use diaphragm liquid pumps for self-priming transfer, dispensing, ink, and water systems.
  • Use piston pumps or mini compressors when the device needs higher pressure or stronger airflow.
  • Use accessories to control noise, filtration, tubing loss, and module reliability.
