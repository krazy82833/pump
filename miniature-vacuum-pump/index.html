Miniature Vacuum Pumps

Miniature vacuum pumps provide stable negative pressure for devices where suction response, noise, lifetime, and compact installation are critical.

Engineering Range

Pump typesDiaphragm, piston, selected compact vacuum platforms
Vacuum range-30 to -85 kPa
Flow range0.3-45 L/min depending on platform
Voltage3V to 24V common options
MediaClean air and gas paths
IntegrationPump-only or pump plus tubing, silencer, and filter module

Typical Applications

  • Vacuum sampling
  • IVD and diagnostics
  • Dental suction
  • Vacuum lifters
  • Aesthetic suction
  • Environmental monitoring

Selection Notes

  • Define target vacuum at the device inlet, not only at the pump port.
  • Check startup under real load and filter resistance.
  • Use shock absorption and silencers when the final device is noise sensitive.
